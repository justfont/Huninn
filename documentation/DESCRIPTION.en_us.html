Huninn is an open-source Traditional Chinese rounded typeface designed by justfont.

Huninn is based on the Japanese existing open-source font Kosugi Maru and the Latin characters were replaced with Varela Round, to ensure a cohesive and high-quality bilingual experience.

The name Huninn (粉圓) is inspired by the Taiwanese term for tapioca pearls, reflecting a playful nod to local culture. Huninn includes commonly used characters in Taiwan, Bopomofo (Zhuyin) symbols, and even adds Taigi and Hokkien phonetic symbols and characters to meet local requirements.

Discover the story behind Huninn typeface in Huninn: A Free and Open-Source Font for Traditional Chinese
